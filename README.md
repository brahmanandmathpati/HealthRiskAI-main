# AI Health Risk Prediction System
# 🏥 HealthRisk AI

### Healthcare Risk Analytics for Insurance, Hospital Finance & Pharmaceutical Investments

## 📌 Overview

HealthRisk AI is a dual-domain Artificial Intelligence platform that combines Healthcare Analytics and Financial Risk Modelling to help insurers, healthcare institutions, pharmaceutical investors, and ESG analysts make better data-driven decisions.

The system integrates clinical outcome prediction, epidemiological modelling, patient risk stratification, pharmaceutical efficacy analysis, and healthcare intelligence directly into financial risk models.

HealthRisk AI also includes **HealthRisk Lab**, a gamified simulation platform where users manage healthcare investments and insurance portfolios while responding to real-world healthcare and financial shocks.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Healthcare and finance are deeply interconnected, yet traditional financial models rarely utilize clinical intelligence.

Insurance companies price policies using historical claims data.
Hospital lenders assess creditworthiness using financial statements.
Pharmaceutical investors evaluate companies using market indicators.

HealthRisk AI bridges this gap by embedding healthcare intelligence directly into financial decision-making.
